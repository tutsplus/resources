Text Sizes/Font Family

If you’d prefer to set up your own slide template, here are the details you’ll need:

Heading 1
Helvetica Bold at 51px with a line-height of 52px.

Heading 2
Helvetica Bold at 28px with a line-height of 26px.

Heading 3
Helvetica Bold at 21px with a line-height of 26px.

Paragraph
Helvetica Regular at 16px with a line-height of 26px (1.625em).

Supporting text
Helvetica Regular at 12px with a line-height of 26px.

A good colour for all text would be #2a3745

A good colour for the background (to match the slide templates) would be #f8f4e7
